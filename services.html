Cargo Services

Comprehensive cargo handling services for all types of goods with specialized equipment and experienced personnel.

Dry Bulk Cargo

From grains to coal and from rice to maize, dry bulk cargoes cover a range of produce and raw materials. Examples: Rice, wheat, maize, beans, fertilizer, sugar, cement, sodium nitrate, clinkers, gypsum, copper slugs, coal, iron ore and zinc ores.

Break Bulk Cargo

Goods that must be loaded individually, including containers. Examples: Iron, steel, metals, motor vehicles & parts, trailers & parts, agri-products, machinery, copper, railway vehicles, tractor & tractor parts, containers.

Bulk Liquid

Petroleum products, kerosene, diesel, jet oil, and edible oil.

Harbor Services

The safety of your ship as it enters any of our ports is one of our top priorities. We offer comprehensive services to ensure safe vessel operations.

Pilotage

Vessels Traffic System (VTS) Services, Piloting vessels in and out Port available for 24 hours for Container, ROR, and General cargo vessels. KOJ Vessels berthing and unberthing only day light, SPM Vessels berthing only daylight and unberthing at any time.

Mooring/Unmooring

Mooring/Unmooring vessel at Berth using mooring boats along the Berth, service boat and one tug available for mooring SPM Vessel.

Tugging

There are Four (4) berthing tugs including A50 55 Bollard pull available. Assisting vessels maneuvering in and out with two tugs. Towing vessels.

Support Services

Comprehensive support services ensuring safety, security, and emergency response capabilities for all port operations.

Police and Fire

Our police and fire teams are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to respond to any emergency at the port and neighboring areas. The fully functional and well equipped fire brigade consists of experienced firemen and rescue professionals.

Ambulance and Medical Response

MPA's medical response team are trained to handle any medical emergencies around the port areas. There is also a 24-hour ambulance service with an on the clock first responders team.

Pollution Control

Pollution control at the berths to ensure environmental compliance and protection.
